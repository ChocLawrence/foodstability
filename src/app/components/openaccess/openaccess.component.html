Open Access Policy

Open access articles (pdf) and journals are identified by the orange open lock icon.



The research papers published in the Journal of Food Stability are fully open access. They are immediately freely available to read, download, use and distributed. The Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License permits commercial and non-commercial re-use of an open access paper, as long as the author is properly attributed. Copyright on any research paper published by FTD Resources Publisher Open Access Journals is retained by the author(s). Authors grant FTD Resources publisher to publish their paper and identify itself as the original publisher. They also grant free access and use of the paper by a third party as long as the original authors, publication date; volume, issue and publisher are identified.
